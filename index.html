Pilot version

Is your business AI-ready?

Small and medium-sized enterprises across G7 economies adopt AI at significantly lower rates than large companies, despite its potential to drive productivity and growth. Answer a few questions to identify your AI readiness profile — assessed against the OECD taxonomy for SME AI adoption — and discover government support available to your business. An OECD tool developed under the 2026 French G7 Presidency.

🔒 No data stored · ⏱ ~5 minutes to complete · 🌐 Available in English

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this tool for? ▸
This tool is designed for owners and managers of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in G7 countries — whether your business is already using AI, actively considering it, or has not yet started. No technical expertise is required.
How long does it take? ▸
The questionnaire takes approximately five minutes to complete. It covers your business profile, your current or planned use of AI, and the main obstacles you face in adopting or scaling it.
What happens to my data? ▸
No data is collected, stored, or transmitted. All responses are processed locally within your browser and are never sent to any server.
What will I get at the end? ▸
At the end of the questionnaire, you will receive a profile of your business's AI readiness — indicating where you currently stand and what that implies for your next steps — along with a curated selection of government support programmes and initiatives available in your country.
How was this tool developed? ▸
This tool was developed by the OECD under the 2026 French G7 Presidency, building on the SME AI Adoption Blueprint adopted under the 2025 Canadian G7 Presidency. It applies the OECD taxonomy for SME AI readiness to map businesses to a profile and match them to relevant support. Its development draws on the evidence base and policy networks of the OECD.AI Policy Observatory and the OECD's Digital for SME (D4SME) Global Initiative.
